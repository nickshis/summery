Mother Teresa was born 1910 in Skopje in Albania. She spend her life for helping poor people. She got the Nobel Prize in 1979. Mother Teresa was famous for saying, "Not all of us can do great things, but we can do small things with great love". Mother Teresa has a lot of followers that helps sick or poor people. Mother Teresa left a mark in the history of the people, how one people can change world with their good works
